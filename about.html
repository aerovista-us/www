About

Some DJs chase perfection. I chase the moment right before the crowd realizes it’s too late.

The origin story

It started with a borrowed controller, one blown speaker, and a dream: “What if I committed to the bit… forever?” Since then: weddings, basements, clubs, and at least one corporate retreat where I was asked to “please stop.”

Translation: I’m a comedic performance DJ with real chops *sometimes* — built for energy, crowd work, and chaotic fun.

What you actually get

  • High-energy set design (clean where it matters)
  • Funny, branded moments (“World’s Worst” gimmick)
  • Requests handled respectfully… and occasionally ignored
  • Optional mic hype
  • A show people remember

FAQ

Are you actually bad?

Not always. The “worst” is the brand — the performance is real. Think: comedy + energy + club polish.

What genres?

Trap, hip-hop, EDM, throwbacks, jersey, baile funk, pop chaos. We tailor to the crowd.

Do you bring gear?

Depends on the venue. I can be plug-and-play or full setup. Put it in the booking notes.
